  • Course Curriculum

    • Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is one of the most important technologies of today. It allows machines to think, learn, and work like humans. We can see AI in our daily lives through voice assistants, face unlock in mobiles, and online shopping suggestions. Today, AI is used in almost every field. In education, smart learning apps use AI to guide students. In healthcare, AI helps doctors in diagnosing diseases at an early stage. Farmers also use AI for weather prediction and better crop management. Banks use AI for safe online transactions, and traffic departments use it for smoother vehicle movement. Even in translation and communication, AI is very useful. But AI has both advantages and disadvantages. It makes life easier, saves time, and reduces human errors. At the same time, there is fear that AI may replace human jobs and reduce employment. Also, if misused, it can harm human skills, privacy and security.
